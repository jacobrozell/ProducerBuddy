Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 June 2026

MixStack is designed to keep your music and data private.

What we collect

Nothing by default. MixStack does not collect, transmit, or sell personal information. The App Store build contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Dogfood builds may optionally enable anonymous, allowlisted analytics events when launched with -enable_analytics. Those events never include song titles, file names, or audio content.

Where your data lives

The audio files you import and the metadata you create (songs, mixes, projects, BPM/key, notes) are stored only on your device, inside the app's private storage. They are included in your device/iCloud backups if you have those enabled, under Apple's terms.

Sharing

When you use a share action (text, tracklist, or a release card image), content is handed to the system share sheet and goes only to the destination you choose. MixStack does not see or retain it.

Microphone

If a future version uses the microphone to record audio, iOS will ask your permission first, and recordings are stored on-device like any other audio.

Children

MixStack does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.
