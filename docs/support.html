FAQ

How do I add music?

On the Library tab, tap + → Import Audio… and pick one or more files. MixStack creates a song per file and estimates the BPM and key automatically — you can correct anything by editing the song.

The detected BPM or key is wrong.

Detection is an estimate. Open the song, tap Edit, and set the correct value. You can also re-run detection from the song's menu.

How does track sequencing work?

Add songs to a project, then drag to reorder. Each track shows whether its energy rises or falls versus the previous one, flags abrupt tempo jumps and key clashes, and the energy chart plots the arc of your record. "Suggest Order" proposes a build-to-a-peak sequence.

Where are my files stored?

On your device only. See the privacy policy.
